About

Who We Are?

At SMILORA, our mission is to make dental care simple, comfortable, trustworthy, women and children friendly. The clinic is founded by Dr. Ayesha Fathima, who has years of experience as a resident dentist at multiple clincis and large hospitals. With this experience, she brings both clinical expertise and a personal touch to every patient’s care.

What We Do?

We provide a full range of dental services — from routine check-ups and preventive care to treatments such as orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and implants. At SMILORA, we focus not just on healthy teeth, but on creating a relaxed, patient-friendly experience. Every smile we treat is cared for with the same dedication and attention we’d give to our own family.
